Benefits of AI in Healthcare







The beauty of artifical intelligence is that it can potentially be connected to multiple areas of study. Not just what the general public thinks. An area that I learned about recently that is effected by AI just as any other area is healthcare/medicine. The main use of this technology is essentially to make test dummies from AI that would be layed out as if they were human. This would be beneficial because it would mean no human would be necessary to test new medicine and understand diseases. In summary,"...complex algorithms and software to emulate human cognition in the analysis of complicated medical data. Specifically, AI is the ability for computer algorithms to approximate conclusions without direct human input."
